Introduction: Surety Bond Forms

Should the defendant ‘Fail to Appear’ on a case with a Surety Bond, there are a number of forms relating to a ‘Bondman’s Process’ that may be used if requested by the Professional Surety.

Should the Defendant still not be presented, there are several forms relating to ‘Conditional Forfeiture’ and ‘Final Forfeiture’.

These forms are found in the Bond Surety Forms Menu.

Navigating to the Surety Bond Forms

The forms require a Surety Bond record to first be created as previously demonstrated.

Go to any of the Defendant’s Cases in the Case Table (or the actual Surety Bond). Select the appropriate form from the Surety Bond Menu and press enter.
